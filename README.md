# Blockchain-Based Sustainable Manufacturing and Production Platform

## Overview

This platform leverages blockchain technology to ensure transparency, accountability, and sustainability across the entire manufacturing lifecycle. The system consists of five interconnected smart contracts that address key aspects of sustainable manufacturing.

## Smart Contracts

### 1. Circular Design Implementation Contract (`circular-design.clar`)
- Ensures products are designed for reuse, repair, and recycling
- Tracks product lifecycle stages and sustainability metrics
- Manages design certifications and compliance scores

### 2. Manufacturing Waste Reduction Contract (`waste-reduction.clar`)
- Minimizes waste and maximizes resource efficiency in production
- Tracks waste generation, recycling rates, and efficiency improvements
- Implements waste reduction targets and rewards

### 3. Sustainable Material Sourcing Contract (`material-sourcing.clar`)
- Verifies that raw materials are obtained through responsible practices
- Manages supplier certifications and material traceability
- Tracks environmental impact of sourcing decisions

### 4. Energy Efficiency Optimization Contract (`energy-efficiency.clar`)
- Reduces energy consumption in manufacturing processes
- Monitors energy usage patterns and optimization opportunities
- Implements energy efficiency targets and carbon footprint tracking

### 5. Worker Safety and Rights Contract (`worker-safety.clar`)
- Ensures safe working conditions and fair labor practices
- Tracks safety incidents, training completion, and compliance metrics
- Manages worker rights verification and reporting

## Key Features

- **Transparency**: All manufacturing data is recorded on-chain for full transparency
- **Accountability**: Smart contracts enforce compliance with sustainability standards
- **Incentivization**: Reward mechanisms for meeting sustainability targets
- **Traceability**: Complete supply chain visibility from raw materials to end products
- **Compliance**: Automated verification of regulatory and certification requirements

## Data Structures

Each contract maintains comprehensive data structures for:
- Entity registration and verification
- Metric tracking and historical data
- Compliance scoring and certification status
- Reward and penalty mechanisms

## Getting Started

1. Deploy contracts to Stacks blockchain
2. Register manufacturing entities and suppliers
3. Begin tracking sustainability metrics
4. Monitor compliance and performance dashboards

## Testing

The platform includes comprehensive test suites using Vitest to ensure contract reliability and security.
